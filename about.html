Who We Are

Leading Automotive Component Manufacturer

Saishwar Automotive Industries is a premier manufacturer of precision machined components and sub-assemblies for the automotive industry. Located in Chakan MIDC, Pune, we have been serving the automotive sector with excellence for over 15 years.

Our state-of-the-art facility is equipped with advanced CNC machines, VMC, lathes, and specialized equipment to deliver high-quality components that meet international standards. We specialize in manufacturing brass bushes, guide bushes, pistons, pipes, quarter pins, and custom precision parts.

Our commitment to quality, timely delivery, and customer satisfaction has made us a trusted partner for leading automotive manufacturers across India.
